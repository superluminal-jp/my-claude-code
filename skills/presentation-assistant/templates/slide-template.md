## Global deck rules

- **Fonts:** max 2 families; body ≥18pt; title ~32pt.  
- **Margins:** ~1 inch consistent.  
- **Slide numbers:** bottom right.  
- **Sources:** bottom left, readable.  

Do **not** put methodology **brand names** on slides unless the client explicitly asks.
